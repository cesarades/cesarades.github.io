Projects

For the 2021 summer, I spent nine weeks in London at an intensive Web Development Bootcamp called Le Wagon. Here I learned core Full-Stack skills such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, SQL, git, GitHub, Heroku, and Ruby on Rails. Through the course, I created two web applications from scratch which I outline below. Both applications are seeded with mock data.

A platform that connects volunteers with their local communities. As an organization, you can post upcoming events for people to sign up for, and as a volunteer, you sign up for those awesome events! The web application offers multiple features including instant messaging, filters, maps, and more.

A web application that acts as a marketplace for extreme sports equipment. This platform intends to replicate that of Airbnb, except with a different product: equipment as opposed to housin. It implements features such as renting equipment, listing items, and more.
